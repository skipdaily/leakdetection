How It Works

Learn about our professional leak detection process and what to expect when you work with us.

Our Detection Process

Using advanced technology and expert knowledge to find and fix leaks before they cause major damage.

Our Step-by-Step Process

1

Schedule Your Inspection

Begin by scheduling a leak detection appointment online or by phone. We'll find a time that works for your schedule.

The scheduling process is quick and easy, typically taking less than 5 minutes to complete.

2

Professional Home Assessment

Our certified technicians arrive at your home and conduct a thorough assessment of your property, focusing on high-risk areas for leaks.

Our technicians use non-invasive techniques that won't damage your property. The initial assessment typically takes 30-45 minutes.

3

Advanced Leak Detection

We deploy specialized equipment including acoustic sensors, thermal imaging cameras, and gas detection tools to precisely locate any leaks.

Water Leak Detection

Using acoustic technology to detect water leaks behind walls and under floors.

Gas Leak Detection

Advanced sensors that can detect even tiny amounts of gas in the air.

Carbon Monoxide Testing

Special equipment to identify sources of carbon monoxide in your home.

4

Detailed Report & Recommendations

After the inspection, we provide a comprehensive report detailing any leaks found, their severity, and our recommended solutions.

Our reports include photos, diagrams, and clear explanations so you fully understand the situation and your options.

5

Solution Implementation

If repair services are needed, we can either perform the repairs ourselves or coordinate with trusted partners to ensure your leaks are fixed properly.

We offer ongoing monitoring solutions to provide you with peace of mind and early detection of any future leaks.

Our Technology

Acoustic Leak Detection

Our acoustic sensors can detect the sound of water leaking behind walls, under floors, and in pipes that aren't visible. This technology allows us to pinpoint water leaks with 99.7% accuracy.

  • Non-invasive detection
  • Pinpoint accuracy
  • No property damage

Thermal Imaging

Our infrared thermal imaging cameras can detect temperature differences that indicate the presence of water, helping us visualize areas that may be affected by leaks that aren't yet visible to the naked eye.

  • Detects hidden moisture
  • Visualizes problem areas
  • Early problem detection

Gas Detection Sensors

Our specialized gas detection equipment can identify even small amounts of natural gas, propane, or other combustible gases that could pose a serious safety risk to your home and family.

  • Highly sensitive detection
  • Identifies source locations
  • Prevents dangerous situations

Carbon Monoxide Detectors

Our advanced carbon monoxide detection equipment can identify sources of this deadly, odorless gas in your home, helping protect your family from this silent killer.

  • Identifies CO sources
  • Measures concentration levels
  • Life-saving early detection

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical leak inspection take?

Most inspections take 1-2 hours, depending on the size of your property and the type of inspection needed.

Will leak detection damage my property?

No, our methods are non-invasive. We use technology that can detect leaks without requiring any holes or damage to your walls, floors, or ceilings.

How often should I have my home checked for leaks?

We recommend annual inspections for preventative purposes, or immediately if you notice any signs of potential leaks such as water stains, musty odors, or higher than normal utility bills.

Do you fix the leaks you find?

Yes, in many cases we can repair the leaks we detect. For more complex repairs, we can coordinate with specialized contractors to ensure the issue is resolved properly.
